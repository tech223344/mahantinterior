Our Services

Wooden Furniture

UPVC Furniture
UPVC (Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride) furniture is made from a strong, weather-resistant plastic material. It does not rust, warp, or get damaged by water, making it ideal for kitchen cabinets, wardrobes, office furniture, bathroom vanities, and outdoor furniture. It is durable, termite-proof, waterproof, fire-retardant, and low-maintenance. UPVC furniture also comes in various colors and designs, giving a modern and clean look.

POP
POP is a fine, white powder used mainly for ceiling designs, wall décor, cornices, partitions, and interior finishing work. It is easy to mold into any shape and gives a smooth, elegant, and premium look to interiors. POP is lightweight, long-lasting, fire-resistant, and perfect for creating modern ceiling designs and decorative elements. It also allows clean finishing and can be painted in any color.

Decor And Lightning
Home décor and lighting include items like wall art, clocks, curtains, lamps, LEDs, chandeliers, and decorative accessories that enhance the look of a space. They add a stylish, modern, and personalized touch to your home. Good décor and lighting are easy to maintain, mood-enhancing, visually appealing, and help create a comfortable and premium atmosphere in every room.

House Colour
House colour refers to interior and exterior wall paints used to give your home a fresh, stylish, and attractive look. It includes shades for bedrooms, hall, kitchen, and outdoor walls, available in matte, gloss, and texture finishes. Good house colours are washable, long-lasting, dust-resistant, fade-proof, and easy to maintain, helping create a clean, bright, and modern atmosphere in every space.

Mirror Work
Mirror work includes decorative mirrors, wall panels, frames, wardrobes mirrors, and designer mirror pieces used to make interiors look bright, stylish, and spacious. It enhances the overall look of living rooms, bedrooms, and hallways. Good mirror work is easy to clean, long-lasting, visually appealing, and adds premium elegance, helping reflect light and create a modern, luxurious atmosphere in any space.

And Many More
“Many more” includes additional interior and exterior services like wallpapers, modular kitchens, PVC panels, glass work, flooring, curtains, blinds, décor items, and renovation work that enhance the overall home design. These help complete the look of any space with a clean, modern, and premium feel. Such services are customizable, durable, easy to maintain, and visually appealing, ensuring a perfect finishing touch to your home’s interior and exterior style.
