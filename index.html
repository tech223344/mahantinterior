Our Services

Wooden Furniture & Interior Solutions

UPVC Furniture
UPVC (Unplasticized Polyvinyl Chloride) furniture is made from a strong, weather-resistant plastic material. It does not rust, warp, or get damaged by water, making it ideal for kitchen cabinets, wardrobes, office furniture, bathroom vanities, and outdoor furniture.

POP False Ceiling & Walls
POP is a fine, white powder used mainly for ceiling designs, wall décor, cornices, partitions, and interior finishing work. It is easy to mold into any shape and gives a smooth, elegant, and premium look to interiors. POP is lightweight, long-lasting, fire-resistant, and perfect for creating modern ceiling designs.

Decor And Lighting
Home décor and lighting include items like wall art, clocks, curtains, lamps, LEDs, chandeliers, and decorative accessories that enhance the look of a space. They add a stylish, modern, and personalized touch to your home.

House Colour & Painting
House colour refers to interior and exterior wall paints used to give your home a fresh, stylish, and attractive look. Good house colours are washable, long-lasting, dust-resistant, fade-proof, and easy to maintain, helping create a clean, bright, and modern atmosphere.

Mirror Work & Glass Panels
Mirror work includes decorative mirrors, wall panels, frames, wardrobes mirrors, and designer mirror pieces used to make interiors look bright, stylish, and spacious. It enhances the overall look of living rooms, bedrooms, and hallways.

And Many More
“Many more” includes additional interior and exterior services like wallpapers, modular kitchens, PVC panels, glass work, flooring, curtains, blinds, décor items, and renovation work that enhance the overall home design.
